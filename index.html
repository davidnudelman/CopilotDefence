    Recipes

    Mythics & Immortals are forged from specific units plus Luck Stones (🔷). Place every ingredient on the board, select one, and merge.

      Controls

      • S Summon
      • R Epic Roulette (60%, 1🔷)
      • L Legendary Roulette (30%, 4🔷)
      • M Merge
      • X Sell
      • 1/2/3 Speed
      • Tap a guardian to inspect, drag to reposition

      Tips

      • Three identical units of the same family merge into the next tier within that family — deterministic.
      • Mythics & Immortals can only be reached by merging — there's no roulette for them.
      • Mythic units charge a Mana ring with each attack. Park them near a Mana Catalyst (Arcane Epic) to charge faster.
      • Safe Box pays a small % of your wave-start gold (capped). Money Gun amplifies ATK based on your pile.
      • Gadgets and Guardians provide permanent power. Upgrade them between runs using Gems and Tokens.
      • Send Rare+ units to the Dungeon to grind extra Luck Stones over time.
